Save Energy and Money

Understanding how much energy your home is using, when and where, empowers you to find savings. Sense gives you the tools to break it down and take action.

Enhanced Security

SWS enables timely detection of anomalies such as theft or leakage in home energy consumption. By promptly alerting homeowners to deviations from normal usage patterns, it helps safeguard their property and resources.

Improved Efficiency

With detailed feedback on electricity usage provided by SWS, homeowners can optimize their energy consumption habits. This leads to reduced waste, lower utility bills, and a more environmentally sustainable lifestyle.

Proactive Monitoring

SWS centralizes home power management, offering real-time monitoring for proactive issue detection. This minimizes disruptions, ensuring smooth energy system operation.
